Cloud Sync Setup

Connect to Firebase to sync tasks across all your devices. Free forever — follow these steps:

Step 1: Go to console.firebase.google.com and sign in with Google.
Step 2: Click "Create a project" (or "Add project"). Name it anything (e.g. "my-tasks"). Disable Google Analytics when asked, then click Create.
Step 3: In the left sidebar, click "Build" → "Realtime Database". Click "Create Database". Choose any location, select "Start in test mode", then click Enable.
Step 4: Copy the database URL shown at the top (looks like https://my-tasks-xxxxx-default-rtdb.firebaseio.com) and paste it below.

Bookmark this page or save this Sync ID — use it on your work computer to see the same tasks.
